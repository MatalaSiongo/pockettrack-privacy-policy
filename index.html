PocketTrack Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Information We Collect

PocketTrack may collect account information such as email addresses and financial records entered by users.

How We Use Information

We use collected information to provide account access, save financial records, generate summaries and improve app functionality.

Firebase Services

PocketTrack uses Firebase Authentication and Firestore provided by Google to securely store user account and application data.

AI Assistant

The AI Assistant processes finance-related questions submitted by users to provide responses and financial insights.

Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures and encrypted connections to protect user data.

Account Deletion

Users can delete their accounts directly within the application. Associated data may be removed from our systems.

Children's Privacy

PocketTrack is not intended for children under 13 years of age.
